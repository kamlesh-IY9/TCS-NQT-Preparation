## CRITICAL PREPARATION RULES

### **Rule 1: Use Past Year Questions ONLY**
- Every concept learned → Practice with TCS NQT past year questions
- Sources: PrepInsta, Testbook, Naukri.com, Talent Battle
- Do NOT solve random online questions (different pattern)

### **Rule 2: Time Management During Practice**
- **Part A**: Numerical (25 min / 20 Q) = 1.25 min/Q
- **Part A**: Reasoning (25 min / 20 Q) = 1.25 min/Q
- **Part A**: Verbal (25 min / 20 Q) = 1.25 min/Q
- **Part B**: Advanced Coding = 15-20 min per problem (2-3 problems in 115 mins)

### **Rule 3: Weakest Areas Get More Time**
Days focused on:
- Day 2: Reasoning (your weakness)
- Days 4-5: Coding (your weakness)
- This leaves more time for what you're already strong at

### **Rule 4: Don't Memorize, Understand Patterns**
- Reasoning: Pattern recognition over memorization
- Coding: Understand algorithm logic, not just code syntax
- Verbal: Improve comprehension, not vocabulary memorization
